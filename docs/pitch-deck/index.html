The Problem 03 / 09
Why this matters

AI agents allocate real capital.
Nobody can verify why.

Today's autonomous agent (scheduled cron)
  • → Receives capital
  • → Black-box LLM produces decision
  • → Signs and broadcasts on-chain
  • → Reports PnL screenshot
No audit trail · No challenge · No accountability.
TuringVault
  • → Receives capital
  • → Three independent models challenge each other
  • → Every decision hashed → IPFS → Mantle
  • → Post-execution proof verification (Discipline Layer)
Refusal-with-proof > profitable execution without one.

For RWA allocation specifically — DAO treasuries, DeFi-native funds, compliance-conscious operators — accountability is not a feature. It's the entire reason RWA assets exist.

Architecture 05 / 09
How a decision flows

Every proposed allocation passes three independent models and three on-chain attestations. Then a fourth gate verifies the execution itself.

1. Analyst
Z.ai GLM-5
Proposes allocation from unified market context (CoinGecko, Nansen MCP, Byreal Perps funding/OI, DeFiLlama TVL, Elfa social attention).
2. Validator
Anthropic Claude 4.6
Adversarial gate. Default REJECT. Burden of proof on the proposer. R/R ≥ 1.5:1 mandatory.
3. Arbiter
Google Gemini 3.5
Tiebreaker on disagreement. 2-of-3 consensus required to execute.
